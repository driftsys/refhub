# Functional Safety

Standards and regulations for ensuring safety of electrical, electronic, and
programmable electronic systems. Covers the foundational IEC 61508 framework and
its sector-specific derivatives for process industry, machinery, nuclear power,
and power drive systems.

**Contents:** [Standard](#standard) | [Regulation](#regulation)

## Standard

- [IEC-60880] Nuclear power plants — Instrumentation and control systems
  important to safety — Software aspects for computer-based systems performing
  category A functions

  Software requirements for the highest safety category (A) in nuclear power
  plants. Specifies rigorous software lifecycle processes, formal methods,
  defensive programming, and independence requirements for reactor protection
  systems.

  Document: IEC 60880:2006+AMD1:2022\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/67281>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: nuclear safety, software safety, category A, reactor protection,
  formal methods, defensive programming\
  Derived-from: IEC-61513

- [IEC-61508] Functional safety of E/E/PE safety-related systems

  The foundational standard for functional safety across all industries. Defines
  Safety Integrity Levels (SIL 1-4) and a generic framework from which
  sector-specific standards derive (ISO 26262, EN 50128, IEC 62304, DO-178C).

  Document: IEC 61508:2010 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/5515>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: functional safety, SIL, safety integrity level, E/E/PE, safety
  lifecycle, safety framework

- [IEC-61511] Functional safety — Safety instrumented systems for the process
  industry

  Sector-specific application of IEC 61508 for the process industry (chemical,
  petrochemical, oil and gas). Defines requirements for safety instrumented
  systems (SIS) including sensors, logic solvers, and final elements.

  Document: IEC 61511:2016 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/24241>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: process industry, SIS, safety instrumented system, chemical,
  petrochemical, oil and gas, logic solver\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [IEC-61513] Nuclear power plants — Instrumentation and control important to
  safety — General requirements for systems

  Defines requirements for I&C systems important to nuclear safety. Specifies
  system architecture, design, qualification, and V&V requirements for
  safety-classified systems in nuclear power plants.

  Document: IEC 61513:2011\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/5533>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: nuclear safety, instrumentation and control, I&C, system
  architecture, qualification, V&V\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [IEC-61800-5-2] Adjustable speed electrical power drive systems — Functional
  safety requirements

  Functional safety requirements for adjustable speed power drive systems.
  Defines safety functions and SIL capability for drive systems used in
  machinery, industrial automation, and process control.

  Document: IEC 61800-5-2:2016\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/25498>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: power drive systems, adjustable speed drives, safety functions,
  machinery, industrial automation\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [IEC-62061] Safety of machinery — Functional safety of safety-related control
  systems

  Application of IEC 61508 to machinery control systems. Defines SIL-based
  requirements for safety-related electrical, electronic, and programmable
  electronic control systems used in machinery.

  Document: IEC 62061:2021 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/62340>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: machinery safety, control systems, SIL, programmable electronic,
  safety-related systems\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [ISO-12100] Safety of machinery — General principles for design

  Fundamental concepts and methodology for achieving safety in machinery design.
  Defines risk assessment and risk reduction strategies. Referenced by all
  sector-specific machinery safety standards.

  Document: ISO 12100:2010\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/51528.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: machinery safety, risk assessment, risk reduction, design
  principles, safety methodology

- [ISO-13849-1] Safety of machinery — Safety-related parts of control systems —
  Part 1

  Performance requirements for safety-related parts of control systems. Defines
  Performance Levels (PL a-e) and Categories (B, 1-4) as an alternative to
  SIL-based assessment for machinery applications.

  Document: ISO 13849-1:2023\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/73481.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: performance level, PL, categories, machinery control, safety-related
  parts, risk assessment\
  Derived-from: ISO-12100

## Regulation

- [EU-Machinery-Regulation] Regulation on machinery products

  EU regulation replacing the Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC. Establishes
  essential health and safety requirements for machinery including digital
  components, AI-based safety functions, and cybersecurity. Introduces mandatory
  third-party conformity assessment for high-risk machinery.

  Document: Regulation (EU) 2023/1230\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2023/1230/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: machinery regulation, EU regulation, conformity assessment, AI
  safety, cybersecurity, high-risk machinery
