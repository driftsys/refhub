# Functional Safety

- [IEC-61508] Functional safety of E/E/PE safety-related systems

  The foundational standard for functional safety across all industries. Defines
  Safety Integrity Levels (SIL 1-4) and a generic framework from which
  sector-specific standards derive (ISO 26262, EN 50128, IEC 62304, DO-178C).

  Document: IEC 61508:2010 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/5515

- [IEC-61511] Functional safety — Safety instrumented systems for the process
  industry

  Sector-specific application of IEC 61508 for the process industry (chemical,
  petrochemical, oil and gas). Defines requirements for safety instrumented
  systems (SIS) including sensors, logic solvers, and final elements.

  Document: IEC 61511:2016 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/24241\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [IEC-62061] Safety of machinery — Functional safety of safety-related control
  systems

  Application of IEC 61508 to machinery control systems. Defines SIL-based
  requirements for safety-related electrical, electronic, and programmable
  electronic control systems used in machinery.

  Document: IEC 62061:2021 (Edition 2.0)\
  URL: https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/62340\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [ISO-12100] Safety of machinery — General principles for design

  Fundamental concepts and methodology for achieving safety in machinery design.
  Defines risk assessment and risk reduction strategies. Referenced by all
  sector-specific machinery safety standards.

  Document: ISO 12100:2010\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/51528.html

- [ISO-13849-1] Safety of machinery — Safety-related parts of control systems —
  Part 1

  Performance requirements for safety-related parts of control systems. Defines
  Performance Levels (PL a-e) and Categories (B, 1-4) as an alternative to
  SIL-based assessment for machinery applications.

  Document: ISO 13849-1:2023\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/73481.html\
  Derived-from: ISO-12100
