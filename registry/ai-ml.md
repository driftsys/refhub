# Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

## Governance

- [ISO-42001] Information technology — Artificial intelligence — Management
  system

  Requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually
  improving an AI management system (AIMS). Addresses responsible AI governance,
  risk management, and transparency for organizations developing or using AI
  systems.

  Document: ISO/IEC 42001:2023\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html>

- [ISO-23894] Information technology — Artificial intelligence — Guidance on
  risk management

  Guidance for managing risks related to AI systems. Extends ISO 31000 risk
  management principles to AI-specific concerns including bias, transparency,
  robustness, and accountability.

  Document: ISO/IEC 23894:2023\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/77304.html>

- [ISO-38507] Information technology — Governance of IT — Governance
  implications of the use of artificial intelligence by organizations

  Guidance for governing bodies on the use of AI. Addresses strategic alignment,
  value delivery, risk optimization, and resource management of AI systems
  within organizational governance frameworks.

  Document: ISO/IEC 38507:2022\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/56641.html>

## Regulation

- [EU-AI-Act] Regulation laying down harmonised rules on artificial intelligence

  European Union regulation establishing a risk-based framework for AI systems.
  Classifies AI by risk level (unacceptable, high, limited, minimal) and imposes
  obligations for conformity assessment, transparency, and human oversight.

  Document: Regulation (EU) 2024/1689\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj>

## Engineering

- [ISO-22989] Information technology — Artificial intelligence — Concepts and
  terminology

  Establishes terminology and concepts for AI. Provides a common vocabulary for
  AI systems, machine learning, neural networks, and related technologies used
  across the ISO/IEC AI standards series.

  Document: ISO/IEC 22989:2022\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/74296.html>

- [ISO-23053] Framework for Artificial Intelligence (AI) Systems Using Machine
  Learning (ML)

  Defines a generic framework for AI systems that use machine learning.
  Describes functional components and their interactions covering data
  pipelines, model engineering, and deployment patterns.

  Document: ISO/IEC 23053:2022\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/74438.html>

- [NIST-AI-RMF] NIST AI Risk Management Framework

  Voluntary framework for managing risks in AI systems across their lifecycle.
  Organized around four functions: Govern, Map, Measure, Manage. Provides
  practical guidance for trustworthy and responsible AI.

  Document: NIST AI 100-1 (2023)\
  URL:
  <https://www.nist.gov/artificial-intelligence/ai-risk-management-framework>

## Ethics

- [IEEE-7000] Model Process for Addressing Ethical Concerns during System Design

  Defines a process for embedding ethical values into system design. Provides a
  systematic approach for identifying stakeholder values, translating them into
  requirements, and validating ethical alignment throughout development.

  Document: IEEE 7000-2021\
  URL: <https://standards.ieee.org/ieee/7000/6781/>

- [UNESCO-AI-Ethics] Recommendation on the Ethics of Artificial Intelligence

  International normative instrument on AI ethics. Establishes values and
  principles including proportionality, fairness, transparency, human oversight,
  and environmental sustainability for AI development and deployment.

  Document: UNESCO (2021)\
  URL: <https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000381137>

## Safety and Trustworthiness

- [ISO-25059] Software engineering — Systems and software Quality Requirements
  and Evaluation (SQuaRE) — Quality model for AI-based systems

  Extends the ISO 25010 software quality model with AI-specific quality
  characteristics including functional adaptability, robustness, transparency,
  and freedom from bias. Provides a framework for evaluating the quality of
  AI-based systems.

  Document: ISO/IEC 25059:2023\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/80655.html>\
  Derived-from: ISO-25010

- [ISO-24029] Artificial intelligence — Assessment of the robustness of neural
  networks

  Multi-part standard for assessing the robustness of neural networks. Covers
  formal methods, statistical approaches, and perturbation-based testing for
  evaluating how neural networks respond to adversarial inputs and
  distributional shifts.

  Document: ISO/IEC 24029 (series, 2021)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/77609.html>

- [ISO-42005] Information technology — Artificial intelligence — AI system
  impact assessment

  Guidance for conducting impact assessments of AI systems. Defines a structured
  process for evaluating societal, environmental, and individual impacts of AI
  deployment including unintended consequences and mitigation measures.

  Document: ISO/IEC 42005:2025\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/44545.html>

## Data Quality

- [ISO-5259] Artificial intelligence — Data quality for analytics and machine
  learning

  Multi-part standard addressing data quality requirements for AI and ML. Covers
  data quality measures, management processes, and requirements for training,
  validation, and test datasets used in machine learning pipelines.

  Document: ISO/IEC 5259 (series, 2024)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/81088.html>
