# Rail

- [EN-50126] Railway applications — The specification and demonstration of
  Reliability, Availability, Maintainability and Safety (RAMS)

  Defines the RAMS lifecycle for railway applications. Provides a systematic
  process for specifying and demonstrating reliability, availability,
  maintainability, and safety across all phases from concept to decommissioning.

  Document: EN 50126-1:2017\
  URL:
  https://standards.iteh.ai/catalog/standards/cen/c1a0a640-5073-4495-9498-63beb9f73475/en-50126-1-2017\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [EN-50128] Railway applications — Software for railway control and protection
  systems

  Defines requirements for development, deployment, and maintenance of software
  for railway control and protection systems. Specifies Software Safety
  Integrity Levels (SSIL 0-4) and required techniques at each level.

  Document: EN 50128:2011+A2:2020\
  URL:
  https://standards.iteh.ai/catalog/standards/cen/45a21a53-e498-4255-b6e9-4f1d8fa1f4b6/en-50128-2011\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [EN-50129] Railway applications — Communication, signalling and processing
  systems — Safety related electronic systems for signalling

  Defines requirements for the acceptance and approval of safety-related
  electronic systems for railway signalling. Specifies evidence required for
  safety case demonstration and independent safety assessment.

  Document: EN 50129:2018\

- [EN-50657] Railway applications — Rolling stock applications — Software on
  board rolling stock

  Software standard specifically for rolling stock applications. Covers on-board
  software for traction, braking, doors, passenger information, and other
  rolling stock subsystems. Complements EN 50128.
