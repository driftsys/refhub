# Environmental Compliance

Regulations and standards governing environmental impact of products and
operations. Covers hazardous substance restrictions, waste and recycling
requirements, product safety marking, sustainability reporting, and equipment
for use in hazardous environments.

**Contents:** [Standard](#standard) | [Regulation](#regulation)

## Standard

- [IEC-60079] Explosive atmospheres

  Multi-part standard defining requirements for electrical equipment in
  explosive atmospheres. Covers protection concepts (Ex d, Ex e, Ex i, Ex p, Ex
  n), equipment marking, installation, inspection, and maintenance.

  Document: IEC 60079 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/591>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: explosive atmospheres, Ex protection, ATEX equipment, hazardous
  areas, gas, dust, intrinsic safety

- [ISO-14040] Environmental management — Life cycle assessment — Principles and
  framework

  Defines the principles and framework for conducting life cycle assessments
  (LCA). Covers goal and scope definition, inventory analysis, impact
  assessment, and interpretation for evaluating environmental impacts of
  products and services.

  Document: ISO 14040:2006\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/37456.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: life cycle assessment, LCA, environmental impact, inventory
  analysis, sustainability, environmental management

## Regulation

- [CPSIA] Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act

  U.S. federal law establishing safety standards for children's products and
  consumer goods. Mandates third-party testing, lead and phthalate limits,
  tracking labels, and CPSC registration for durable infant products.

  Document: Public Law 110-314 (2008)\
  URL:
  https://www.cpsc.gov/Regulations-Laws--Standards/Statutes/The-Consumer-Product-Safety-Improvement-Act\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: consumer product safety, children's products, lead limits,
  phthalates, CPSC, US law, third-party testing

- [EU-ATEX] Directive on equipment and protective systems intended for use in
  potentially explosive atmospheres

  EU directive establishing essential health and safety requirements for
  equipment used in explosive atmospheres (gas, dust, mist). Defines equipment
  groups and categories with corresponding protection levels.

  Document: Directive 2014/34/EU\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2014/34/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: ATEX, explosive atmospheres, EU directive, equipment groups,
  hazardous environments, gas, dust

- [EU-Battery-Regulation] Regulation concerning batteries and waste batteries

  EU regulation establishing sustainability requirements for batteries across
  their lifecycle. Covers carbon footprint declarations, recycled content
  mandates, digital battery passports, due diligence, and end-of-life collection
  and recycling targets.

  Document: Regulation (EU) 2023/1542\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2023/1542/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: batteries, battery passport, carbon footprint, recycled content,
  end-of-life, EU regulation, sustainability

- [EU-CSRD] Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive

  EU directive requiring large companies and listed SMEs to report on
  environmental, social, and governance (ESG) matters using European
  Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS). Mandates limited assurance and
  digital tagging of sustainability information.

  Document: Directive (EU) 2022/2464\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2022/2464/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: sustainability reporting, ESG, ESRS, corporate reporting, EU
  directive, digital tagging, assurance

- [EU-ELV] Directive on end-of-life vehicles

  EU directive establishing requirements for the collection, treatment, reuse,
  and recycling of end-of-life vehicles. Restricts heavy metals in vehicle
  materials and sets recovery and recycling targets for automotive
  manufacturers.

  Document: Directive 2000/53/EC (consolidated)\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2000/53/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: end-of-life vehicles, automotive recycling, heavy metals, recovery
  targets, EU directive, vehicle waste

- [EU-GPSD] General Product Safety Regulation

  EU regulation establishing a general safety requirement for consumer products.
  Requires products placed on the EU market to be safe, mandates traceability,
  and defines recall and market surveillance procedures. Replaces Directive
  2001/95/EC.

  Document: Regulation (EU) 2023/988\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2023/988/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: product safety, consumer products, market surveillance,
  traceability, recall, EU regulation

- [EU-LVD] Directive on the harmonisation of the laws relating to the making
  available on the market of electrical equipment designed for use within
  certain voltage limits

  EU directive establishing safety requirements for electrical equipment
  operating between 50-1000V AC or 75-1500V DC. Covers protection against
  electrical, thermal, mechanical, chemical, and radiation hazards.

  Document: Directive 2014/35/EU\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2014/35/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: low voltage, electrical safety, EU directive, CE marking, voltage
  limits, product safety

- [EU-POP] Regulation on persistent organic pollutants

  EU regulation implementing the Stockholm Convention on persistent organic
  pollutants. Prohibits or restricts the manufacture, placing on the market, and
  use of POPs in products and substances.

  Document: Regulation (EU) 2019/1021\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2019/1021/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: persistent organic pollutants, POPs, Stockholm Convention, chemical
  restrictions, EU regulation

- [EU-REACH] Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of
  Chemicals

  EU regulation requiring manufacturers and importers to register chemical
  substances, evaluate risks, and obtain authorisation for substances of very
  high concern (SVHC). Affects all products containing chemicals placed on the
  EU market.

  Document: Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2006/1907/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: chemical registration, SVHC, REACH, chemical risk, authorisation, EU
  regulation, substance restrictions

- [EU-RoHS] Directive on the restriction of the use of certain hazardous
  substances in electrical and electronic equipment

  EU directive restricting the use of lead, mercury, cadmium, hexavalent
  chromium, PBB, and PBDE in electrical and electronic equipment. Applies to all
  EEE placed on the EU market with limited exemptions for specific applications.

  Document: Directive 2011/65/EU (RoHS 2)\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2011/65/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: hazardous substances, lead-free, RoHS, EEE, substance restrictions,
  EU directive, cadmium, mercury

- [EU-WEEE] Directive on waste electrical and electronic equipment

  EU directive establishing requirements for collection, treatment, recycling,
  and recovery of waste EEE. Mandates producer responsibility, collection
  targets, and proper treatment to prevent hazardous substance release.

  Document: Directive 2012/19/EU (WEEE 2)\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2012/19/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: waste electronics, WEEE, producer responsibility, recycling targets,
  e-waste, EU directive, collection

- [TSCA] Toxic Substances Control Act

  U.S. federal law granting EPA authority to regulate chemical substances in
  commerce. Requires chemical risk evaluations, pre-manufacture notification,
  and reporting of chemical data. Amended by the Lautenberg Act (2016).

  Document: 15 U.S.C. ch. 53\
  URL: <https://www.epa.gov/tsca-inventory>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: toxic substances, chemical regulation, EPA, pre-manufacture
  notification, US law, Lautenberg Act
