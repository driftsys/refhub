# Energy and Power Systems

Standards and regulations for electrical power generation, transmission,
distribution, and consumption. Covers smart grid communication (IEC 61850),
SCADA protocols, renewable energy systems (wind, photovoltaic), nuclear power
plant instrumentation and control, and energy market regulation.

**Contents:** [Standard](#standard) | [Regulation](#regulation)

## Standard

- [IEC-60870-5] Telecontrol equipment and systems — Transmission protocols

  Defines communication protocols for telecontrol (SCADA) in power systems. Part
  101 covers serial point-to-point, Part 104 covers TCP/IP networking. The
  dominant SCADA protocol for electrical utility control centres.

  Document: IEC 60870-5 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/3750>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: scada, telecontrol, protection

- [IEC-60880] Nuclear power plants — Instrumentation and control systems
  important to safety — Software aspects for computer-based systems performing
  category A functions

  Software requirements for the highest safety category (A) in nuclear power
  plants. Specifies rigorous software lifecycle processes, formal methods,
  defensive programming, and independence requirements for reactor protection
  systems.

  Document: IEC 60880:2006+AMD1:2022\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/67281>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: nuclear, software safety\
  Derived-from: IEC-61513

- [IEC-61400] Wind energy generation systems

  Comprehensive standard for wind turbine design, testing, and operation. Covers
  design requirements (-1), power performance (-12), acoustic noise (-11),
  electrical characteristics (-21), and full-scale structural testing (-23).

  Document: IEC 61400 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/5426>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: renewable energy, wind

- [IEC-61513] Nuclear power plants — Instrumentation and control important to
  safety — General requirements for systems

  Defines requirements for I&C systems important to nuclear safety. Specifies
  system architecture, design, qualification, and V&V requirements for
  safety-classified systems in nuclear power plants.

  Document: IEC 61513:2011\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/5533>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: nuclear, functional safety\
  Derived-from: IEC-61508

- [IEC-61850] Communication networks and systems for power utility automation

  Comprehensive standard for communication in substations and power utility
  automation. Defines data models, communication protocols (GOOSE, MMS, SV), and
  system engineering processes enabling interoperability between multi-vendor
  protection, control, and monitoring equipment.

  Document: IEC 61850 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/6028>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: smart grid, substation automation

- [IEC-61968] Application integration at electric utilities — System interfaces
  for distribution management

  Defines information exchange interfaces for distribution management systems.
  Covers common information model (CIM), message types, and integration patterns
  for meter data, outage management, and asset management.

  Document: IEC 61968 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/6200>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: smart grid, distribution management, CIM

- [IEC-62138] Nuclear power plants — Instrumentation and control important for
  safety — Software aspects for computer-based systems performing category B or
  C functions

  Software requirements for lower safety categories (B and C) in nuclear power
  plants. Provides a graded approach with less stringent requirements than IEC
  60880 while still ensuring adequate software quality and reliability.

  Document: IEC 62138:2018\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/32050>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: nuclear, software safety\
  Derived-from: IEC-61513

- [IEC-62351] Power systems management and associated information exchange —
  Data and communications security

  Defines cybersecurity requirements for power system communication protocols.
  Covers authentication, access control, and encryption for IEC 61850, IEC
  60870-5, and IEC 61968/61970 to protect critical power infrastructure from
  cyber threats.

  Document: IEC 62351 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/6905>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: smart grid, cybersecurity

- [IEC-62446] Photovoltaic (PV) systems — Requirements for testing,
  documentation and maintenance

  Defines requirements for system documentation, commissioning tests, and
  periodic inspections of grid-connected photovoltaic systems. Covers I-V curve
  tracing, insulation resistance, and performance verification.

  Document: IEC 62446 (series, 2020)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/7046>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: renewable energy, photovoltaic

- [IEEE-2030] Guide for Smart Grid Interoperability of Energy Technology and
  Information Technology Operation

  Provides guidelines for smart grid interoperability covering power systems,
  communications, and information technology interfaces. Defines reference
  models for integrating distributed energy resources, storage, and demand
  response.

  Document: IEEE 2030-2011\
  URL: <https://standards.ieee.org/ieee/2030/5765/>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: smart grid, interoperability

- [IEEE-C37-118] Synchrophasor Measurements for Power Systems

  Defines synchrophasor measurement standards for wide-area monitoring and
  control of power systems. Specifies phasor measurement unit (PMU) data
  formats, accuracy requirements, and communication protocols enabling real-time
  grid state estimation.

  Document: IEEE C37.118 (series)\
  URL: <https://standards.ieee.org/ieee/C37.118.1/4902/>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: synchrophasor, protection, monitoring

## Regulation

- [EU-Electricity-Directive] Directive on common rules for the internal market
  for electricity

  EU directive establishing common rules for electricity generation,
  transmission, distribution, and supply. Covers market access, consumer rights,
  smart metering, demand response, and network codes for interoperability.

  Document: Directive (EU) 2019/944\
  URL: <https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2019/944/oj>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: electricity market, smart metering, EU

- [NRC-10-CFR-50] Domestic Licensing of Production and Utilization Facilities

  U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission regulation governing the licensing of
  nuclear power plants. Defines safety requirements, design criteria, quality
  assurance, and inspection requirements for nuclear facilities.

  Document: 10 CFR Part 50\
  URL: <https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-10/chapter-I/part-50>\
  Label: Regulation\
  Keywords: nuclear, licensing, US
