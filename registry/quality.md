# Quality

- [ISO-9001] Quality management systems — Requirements

  The international standard for quality management systems (QMS). Defines
  requirements for organizations to demonstrate ability to consistently provide
  products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

  Document: ISO 9001:2015\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html

- [ISO-90003] Software engineering — Guidelines for the application of ISO 9001
  to computer software

  Guidance for applying ISO 9001 to software development and maintenance. Maps
  QMS requirements to software engineering practices.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 90003:2018\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/74348.html>\
  Derived-from: ISO-9001

- [CMMI] Capability Maturity Model Integration

  Process improvement framework for developing and maintaining products and
  services. Defines maturity levels (1-5) and capability levels across process
  areas. Widely used in defense, aerospace, and government contracting.

  Document: CMMI V3.0 (2023)\
  URL: https://cmmiinstitute.com/cmmi

- [ISO-33001] Information technology — Process assessment — Concepts and
  terminology

  Defines concepts, terminology, and framework for process assessment. The
  current normative basis for process capability determination. Replacement for
  ISO/IEC 15504.

  Document: ISO/IEC 33001:2015\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/54175.html

- [ISO-9000] Quality management systems — Fundamentals and vocabulary

  Defines fundamental concepts, principles, and vocabulary for quality
  management systems. Provides the conceptual foundation referenced by ISO 9001
  and all related QMS standards.

  Document: ISO 9000:2015\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/45481.html

- [ISO-17025] General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration
  laboratories

  Requirements for laboratories to demonstrate competence, impartiality, and
  consistent operation. Covers method validation, measurement uncertainty,
  equipment calibration, and quality assurance of test results.

  Document: ISO/IEC 17025:2017\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/66912.html

- [ISO-19011] Guidelines for auditing management systems

  Guidance on auditing management systems including planning, conducting, and
  following up on audits. Applicable to quality, environmental, safety, and
  other management system audits.

  Document: ISO 19011:2018\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/70017.html

## Management Systems

- [ISO-14001] Environmental management systems — Requirements with guidance for
  use

  International standard for environmental management systems (EMS). Defines
  requirements for identifying environmental aspects, setting objectives, and
  continually improving environmental performance. Certifiable management system
  standard.

  Document: ISO 14001:2015\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html

- [ISO-45001] Occupational health and safety management systems — Requirements
  with guidance for use

  International standard for occupational health and safety (OH&S) management
  systems. Defines requirements for eliminating hazards and minimising OH&S
  risks. Replaces OHSAS 18001.

  Document: ISO 45001:2018\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html

- [ISO-31000] Risk management — Guidelines

  Provides principles and guidelines for managing risk in any context. Defines
  the risk management process (identification, analysis, evaluation, treatment)
  and framework. Referenced by sector-specific risk standards across all
  domains.

  Document: ISO 31000:2018\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html

## Configuration and Change Management

- [ISO-10007] Quality management — Guidelines for configuration management

  Guidance on applying configuration management within an organization. Defines
  processes for configuration identification, control, status accounting, and
  audit applicable to products, services, and projects.

  Document: ISO 10007:2017\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/70400.html
