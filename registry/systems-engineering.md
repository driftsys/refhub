# Systems Engineering

- [ISO-15288] Systems and software engineering — System life cycle processes

  Defines processes for managing and executing the engineering of systems
  throughout their life cycle. Foundation for V-model development in
  safety-critical industries.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288:2023\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/81702.html

- [ISO-29148] Systems and software engineering — Requirements engineering

  Defines processes and products for requirements engineering across the system
  and software life cycle. Covers stakeholder requirements, system requirements,
  and software requirements.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148:2018\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/72089.html

- [ISO-42010] Systems and software engineering — Architecture description

  Defines a conceptual model for architecture descriptions. Establishes
  viewpoints, views, and concerns as the basis for documenting system and
  software architectures.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 42010:2022\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/74393.html

- [ISO-15939] Systems and software engineering — Measurement process

  Defines a measurement process applicable to system and software engineering.
  Provides a framework for defining measures, collecting data, and using
  measurement results to support decision-making.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 15939:2017\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/71197.html

- [ISO-16085] Systems and software engineering — Life cycle processes — Risk
  management

  Defines processes for managing risk in system and software life cycles.
  Applicable to any organization involved in development, operation, or
  maintenance of systems.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 16085:2021\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/74371.html
