# Systems Engineering

- [ISO-15288] Systems and software engineering — System life cycle processes

  Defines processes for managing and executing the engineering of systems
  throughout their life cycle. Foundation for V-model development in
  safety-critical industries.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288:2023\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/81702.html

- [ISO-29148] Systems and software engineering — Requirements engineering

  Defines processes and products for requirements engineering across the system
  and software life cycle. Covers stakeholder requirements, system requirements,
  and software requirements.

  Document: ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148:2018\
  URL: https://www.iso.org/standard/72089.html
