# Thermal and Climate

## Environmental Testing

- [IEC-60068] Environmental testing

  Comprehensive series defining environmental test methods for electronic and
  electrical products. Covers cold (-2-1), dry heat (-2-2), damp heat (-2-78),
  vibration (-2-6), shock (-2-27), salt mist (-2-52), and combined
  climatic/dynamic tests. Referenced by most product qualification standards.

  Document: IEC 60068 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/489>

- [ISO-16750] Road vehicles — Environmental conditions and testing for
  electrical and electronic equipment

  Multi-part standard defining environmental test conditions for automotive
  electrical and electronic equipment. Covers temperature, humidity, vibration,
  mechanical shock, chemical exposure, dust, and water ingress. See
  semiconductor domain for full entry.

  Document: ISO 16750 (series, 2023)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/82127.html>

## Thermal Comfort

- [ISO-7730] Ergonomics of the thermal environment — Analytical determination
  and interpretation of thermal comfort using calculation of the PMV and PPD
  indices

  Defines the Predicted Mean Vote (PMV) and Predicted Percentage of Dissatisfied
  (PPD) indices for thermal comfort assessment. Covers air temperature, radiant
  temperature, humidity, air velocity, metabolic rate, and clothing insulation.

  Document: ISO 7730:2005\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/39155.html>

- [ISO-7243] Ergonomics of the thermal environment — Assessment of heat stress
  using the WBGT (wet bulb globe temperature) index

  Defines the Wet Bulb Globe Temperature method for assessing heat stress in
  occupational environments. Specifies measurement procedures, reference values,
  and work/rest schedules to prevent heat-related illness in industrial and
  outdoor work settings.

  Document: ISO 7243:2017\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/67188.html>

- [ASHRAE-55] Thermal Environmental Conditions for Human Occupancy

  ASHRAE standard defining acceptable thermal conditions in buildings. Specifies
  comfort zones, adaptive models for naturally ventilated spaces, and local
  discomfort criteria (draught, radiant asymmetry, floor temperature). The
  primary reference for HVAC design.

  Document: ASHRAE Standard 55-2023\
  URL:
  <https://www.ashrae.org/technical-resources/bookstore/standard-55-thermal-environmental-conditions-for-human-occupancy>

## Contact Temperature Safety

- [ISO-13732] Ergonomics of the thermal environment — Methods for the assessment
  of human responses to contact with surfaces

  Multi-part standard defining burn threshold temperatures for contact with hot
  (-1), cold (-3), and moderate-temperature (-2) surfaces. Specifies maximum
  surface temperatures based on material, contact duration, and body part.
  Referenced by machinery and consumer product safety standards.

  Document: ISO 13732 (series, 2006)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/43558.html>

## Building Thermal Performance

- [ISO-6946] Building components and building elements — Thermal resistance and
  thermal transmittance — Calculation method

  Defines calculation methods for thermal resistance (R-value) and thermal
  transmittance (U-value) of building components. Used for energy performance
  calculations, building code compliance, and insulation specification.

  Document: ISO 6946:2017\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/65708.html>

- [ISO-15927] Hygrothermal performance of buildings — Calculation and
  presentation of climatic data

  Multi-part standard providing climatic data and methods for hygrothermal
  building design. Covers reference year weather data, moisture condensation
  risk, and driving rain assessment for building envelope design and simulation.

  Document: ISO 15927 (series)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/69560.html>

## Thermal Management in Electronics

- [JEDEC-JESD51] Thermal Measurement and Characterization Methods for
  Semiconductor Packages

  Defines standard methods for measuring thermal resistance (junction-to- case,
  junction-to-board, junction-to-ambient) of semiconductor packages. Specifies
  test fixtures, environmental conditions, and reporting formats for thermal
  characterisation data sheets.

  Document: JESD51 (series)\
  URL:
  <https://www.jedec.org/category/technology-focus-area/thermal-measurements>

- [IPC-2152] Standard for Determining Current-Carrying Capacity in Printed Board
  Design

  Defines methods for calculating current-carrying capacity of PCB traces based
  on conductor width, thickness, copper weight, and ambient temperature.
  Essential for thermal design of power electronics and high-current board
  layouts.

  Document: IPC-2152 (2009)\
  URL: <https://www.ipc.org/ipc-2152>
