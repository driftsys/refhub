# Semiconductor and Electronics

Standards for semiconductor devices, electronic assemblies, and related testing
covering automotive component qualification, reliability test methods, PCB
fabrication, soldering, and environmental testing for electrical and electronic
equipment.

**Contents:** [Standard](#standard)

## Standard

- [AEC-Q100] Failure Mechanism Based Stress Test Qualification for Integrated
  Circuits

  Industry standard for qualifying integrated circuits for automotive use.
  Defines stress tests organised by failure mechanisms including
  electromigration, gate oxide integrity, hot carrier injection, and NBTI.
  Required by automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers.

  Document: AEC-Q100 Rev. J (2024)\
  URL: <https://www.aecouncil.com/AECDocuments.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: automotive IC qualification, stress testing, electromigration, gate
  oxide integrity, hot carrier injection, NBTI, AEC

- [AEC-Q101] Failure Mechanism Based Stress Test Qualification for Discrete
  Semiconductors

  Qualification standard for discrete semiconductor devices (diodes,
  transistors, thyristors) in automotive applications. Covers junction
  temperature cycling, high-temperature reverse bias, and moisture resistance
  testing.

  Document: AEC-Q101 Rev. E (2021)\
  URL: <https://www.aecouncil.com/AECDocuments.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: discrete semiconductors, automotive qualification, diodes,
  transistors, temperature cycling, moisture resistance, AEC

- [AEC-Q102] Failure Mechanism Based Stress Test Qualification for Discrete
  Optoelectronic Semiconductors in Automotive Applications

  Qualification standard for LEDs and optoelectronic devices used in automotive
  lighting, displays, and sensors. Covers lumen maintenance, colour shift, and
  optical degradation under thermal and moisture stress.

  Document: AEC-Q102 Rev. B (2020)\
  URL: <https://www.aecouncil.com/AECDocuments.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: optoelectronics, LED qualification, automotive lighting, lumen
  maintenance, colour shift, optical degradation, AEC

- [AEC-Q104] Failure Mechanism Based Stress Test Qualification for Multichip
  Modules in Automotive Applications

  Qualification standard for multichip modules (MCMs), system-in-package (SiP),
  and similar multi-die assemblies. Addresses package-level and die-level
  failure mechanisms specific to advanced packaging technologies.

  Document: AEC-Q104 Rev. A (2017)\
  URL: <https://www.aecouncil.com/AECDocuments.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: multichip modules, MCM, system-in-package, SiP, advanced packaging,
  multi-die, automotive qualification, AEC

- [AEC-Q200] Stress Test Qualification for Passive Components

  Qualification standard for passive components (resistors, capacitors,
  inductors, transformers, thermistors) used in automotive electronics. Defines
  test methods and acceptance criteria for environmental and endurance stress.

  Document: AEC-Q200 Rev. E (2022)\
  URL: <https://www.aecouncil.com/AECDocuments.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: passive components, resistors, capacitors, inductors, automotive
  electronics, endurance testing, AEC

- [IEC-60749] Semiconductor devices — Mechanical and climatic test methods

  International standard defining test methods for semiconductor devices. Covers
  bond strength, die shear, solderability, moisture sensitivity, temperature
  cycling, and ESD (HBM, CDM, MM) testing.

  Document: IEC 60749 (series)\
  URL: <https://webstore.iec.ch/en/publication/3371>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: semiconductor testing, bond strength, die shear, ESD, moisture
  sensitivity, temperature cycling, solderability

- [IPC-6012] Qualification and Performance Specification for Rigid Printed
  Boards

  Defines performance and qualification requirements for rigid printed circuit
  boards. Covers base materials, conductor widths, plating thickness, and
  electrical testing for Class 1-3 and automotive/space/military addenda.

  Document: IPC-6012F (2023)\
  URL: <https://www.ipc.org/ipc-6012f>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: rigid PCB, printed circuit board, qualification, plating thickness,
  conductor width, electrical testing

- [IPC-A-610] Acceptability of Electronic Assemblies

  Industry standard defining acceptance criteria for electronic assemblies.
  Covers solder joint quality, component mounting, wire/harness assemblies, and
  cleanliness. Defines Class 1 (general), Class 2 (dedicated service), and Class
  3 (high performance) acceptance levels.

  Document: IPC-A-610H (2024)\
  URL: <https://www.ipc.org/ipc-a-610>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: electronic assembly, solder joint quality, acceptance criteria,
  component mounting, wire harness, inspection

- [IPC-J-STD-001] Requirements for Soldered Electrical and Electronic Assemblies

  Industry standard for soldering requirements. Defines materials, methods, and
  acceptance criteria for hand and machine soldering of through-hole and surface
  mount assemblies at Class 1, 2, and 3 levels.

  Document: IPC J-STD-001H (2024)\
  URL: <https://www.ipc.org/j-std-001h>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: soldering, through-hole, surface mount, solder materials, soldering
  methods, acceptance criteria

- [ISO-16750] Road vehicles — Environmental conditions and testing for
  electrical and electronic equipment

  Multi-part standard defining environmental test conditions for automotive
  electrical and electronic equipment. Covers temperature, humidity, vibration,
  mechanical shock, chemical exposure, dust, and water ingress.

  Document: ISO 16750 (series, 2023)\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/82127.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: automotive environmental testing, temperature, humidity, vibration,
  mechanical shock, chemical exposure, dust, water ingress

- [ISO-20653] Road vehicles — Degrees of protection (IP code) — Protection of
  electrical equipment against foreign objects, water and access

  Defines IP protection ratings for automotive electrical equipment. Extends the
  IEC 60529 IP code system with automotive-specific requirements including
  high-pressure water jets (IPx9K) and road splash exposure.

  Document: ISO 20653:2023\
  URL: <https://www.iso.org/standard/82125.html>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: IP code, ingress protection, automotive IP rating, water protection,
  dust protection, high-pressure water jets

- [JEDEC-JESD22] Reliability Test Methods for Packaged Devices

  Collection of standard test methods for evaluating semiconductor package
  reliability. Covers temperature cycling, thermal shock, moisture sensitivity,
  electromigration, ESD, and latch-up testing.

  Document: JESD22 (series)\
  URL:
  https://www.jedec.org/category/technology-focus-area/reliability-test-methods\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: semiconductor reliability, temperature cycling, thermal shock,
  moisture sensitivity, electromigration, ESD, latch-up, JEDEC

- [JEDEC-JESD47] Stress-Test-Driven Qualification of Integrated Circuits

  Defines a stress-test-driven approach for qualifying new semiconductor
  technologies and products. Specifies minimum sample sizes, stress conditions,
  and failure criteria for reliability qualification.

  Document: JESD47L (2022)\
  URL: <https://www.jedec.org/standards-documents/docs/jesd-47>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: IC qualification, stress testing, reliability qualification, sample
  size, failure criteria, semiconductor qualification, JEDEC

- [MIL-STD-810] Environmental Engineering Considerations and Laboratory Tests

  U.S. military standard defining environmental test methods for equipment.
  Covers temperature, humidity, altitude, vibration, shock, sand/dust, rain,
  salt fog, solar radiation, and fungus. Widely adopted beyond military
  applications.

  Document: MIL-STD-810H (2019)\
  URL: <https://quicksearch.dla.mil/qsDocDetails.aspx?ident_number=35978>\
  Label: Standard\
  Keywords: military environmental testing, temperature, humidity, altitude,
  vibration, shock, sand, dust, rain, salt fog
